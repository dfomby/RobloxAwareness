How Roblox Stands Today

To take into account how dangerous Roblox has become for children, at least six states are currently actively suing Roblox Corporation as of March 2026 for its failure to protect children.

Some of these states include:

Nebraska: the latest state to sue Roblox over child safety failures in March 2026.

(List of States Suing Roblox - Fox News)

Texas: a state that filed a lawsuit against Roblox in November 2025 for Roblox's negligence against predators targeting children on Roblox.

(Texas Roblox Lawsuit Lawyers- Falcon Law Group)

Los Angeles County: a state that filed a lawsuit against Roblox Corporation in February 2026 for endangering and exploiting children.

(LA County Sues Roblox for Unfair and Deceptive Business Practices that Endanger and Exploit Children- lacounty.gov)
