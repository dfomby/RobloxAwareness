What is Roblox?

Roblox is a massive user-generated online gaming platform launched in 2006. Almost all games available on Roblox today were made by users rather than Roblox staff/developers. This platform is most popular among children: roughly half of the over 100 million daily users in America are under the age of 13.

About Us

For years, Roblox has had a serious issue with the lack of action against its online grooming and exploitation of minors. It’s led to multiple kidnappings and grooming situations for children that happen as a result of targeting on Roblox (see our resources page). This issue has been brought up numerous times, from concerned parents to YouTubers. Some states are deciding to sue Roblox as a result of its lack of action. Despite this, Roblox has taken little action to prevent grooming and exploitation on its platform. It’s become a Billionaire Playground for the Roblox corporation to gain money from, and for groomers to run freely.

Roblox Awareness aims to address these issues, raise awareness of how easily children can be exploited, and educate parents on how they can protect their children on Roblox and in online spaces overall.
